Silograms
You will be shown a series of English words, each paired with a nonsense syllable.

There will be 6 word/syllable pairs for you to try to memorize.

After the last word is shown, your job will be to write the English word next to the associated nonsense word, which will be provided for you.
Number Series
You will be given a sequence of numbers, for example (1   3   5   7). Your job will be to determine the pattern (in this case +2) and guess the next number in the sequence (in this case the answer is 9).
